Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 (20 ounce) package prepared macaroni and cheese, thawed if frozen
  • 1 slice American cheese, quartered

Directions

Step 1

Preheat an electric smoker to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C), and add wood chips according to manufacturersâ€™ directions. Use any wood chips that you would use for smoking meat.


Step 2

If prepared macaroni and cheese is not already in a heatproof dish, spoon it into a dish that can sit directly on the rack of a smoker. Divide cheese slices over the top.


Step 3

Place the dish directly on the rack of the smoker, and smoke until hot and bubbly, about 1 1/2 hours. Serve immediately.
